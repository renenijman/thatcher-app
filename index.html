About the Thatcher Effect

The Thatcher effect is a perceptual illusion where a face with inverted features (eyes and mouth) appears normal when viewed as a whole, but becomes grotesque when viewed in parts.

This app uses TensorFlow.js to detect facial landmarks and applies the effect by flipping the eyes and mouth upside down.
